About

"Failure comes only when we forget our ideals and objectives and principles."
- JAWAHARLAL NEHRU
Deforestation and eviction on farmland are the challenging factors due to human's overpopulation and modernisation in the way of living.
True innovation is required for the sophisticated living with no harm to the nature. Disciplined, professionally trained, innovative and focused team of excellence-mShed-change the circumstances to the expectations.

  • APRIL 2016

    "When a great many people are unable to find work, unemployment occurs."
    -CALVIN COOLIDGE

    Found a clue on standing unique by selfemployment. So, bonded a team of 4 Engineering graduates and incorporated our skills in the services like paper to CAD, renovation work, sketchup modeling, and elevation design.

  • AUGUST 2016

    Expansion on Services

    Added on services that were learnt through experience and by the compulsion of clients boosted us to build the company.
    The expanded good turns are Construction documents, interior design solutions, retail space planning, and FF&E.

  • MAY 2017

    As a citizen, you need to know how to be a part of it, how to express yourself- and not just by voting.
    -SANDRA DAY O'CONNOR

    A standard of living in harmony with nature is one among the solutions to maintain peace and cease to corruption.
    As the citizens, mShed will economically serve for the betterment of the people and to the nation through our engineering services like HVAC system design, Electrical system design, Plumbing & Fire Fighting.

  • MARCH 2018

    Transition to Full Service

    "One who shares his knowlegde to the boss becomes employee but one who shares it to the nation becomes a citizen."
    -Deepak Kupendra
    MD, mSheD
    Demands for the land, water and power consumption and higher pollution rate have become an alarming state for the enlightened life of the people.
    Our aim is to develop the land with no loss in material & energy and controlling the pollution through land development design, and consulting on green building and transport & traffic engineering.

2BHK Apartments

Client : Sunway Homes Location : Chennai

Two side elevation design for the apartment with 3 blocks, each block consists of 8 individual homes, parking facility, and a common multipurpose room. Different claddings were added such as stone cladding, high pressure laminates to meet demands of the client. The project was completed in March, 2017

Two Storied Home

Client : Mr.Venugopal Location : Bangalore

A 720 sq.ft individual home was designed for the retired BEL technician by implementing cost effective shera board work and simplicity in colour combination that made luxury effect on the facade. SS railing with glass were implemented to meet transparency for plantation and aesthetic look. The project was completed in February, 2017

3BHK Villa

Client : Ms.Veena Location : Bangalore

A 900sq.ft home was designed to meet the unique solution for the already planned structure. Projections using brick as well as high pressure laminates were incorporated in the design. SS railing and fiber glass were included to meet aesthetic look. The project was completed in 2 months.

Individual Home

Client : Architect Anamalai Location : Chennai

The corner building was designed to make profound frontal impact at the two streets meet. Concrete pergola, concrete projection blocks, tile cladding, and floor finishes were implemented in keeping the ideas of the client. The project was completed in three months.

Two storied Home

Client : Mr.Rowther Naina Location : Chennai

Redesigned the old building of total land area 1500 sq.ft and consisting of parking feasibility of maximum 3. Completely changed the elevation design by implementing terracotta and pergola concept and shera board work in the front facade. Painting colours were also changed accordingly. The overall project cost was about Rs.4,00,000 and completed in two months.

Residential Apartment

Client : Mr.Rahim Location : Tambaram, Chennai

Residential apartment was built to two brothers of about 1200 sq.ft land area consisting of car parking, office room, store room, and two modern flat homes. The two facade elevations were designed after the completion of the structure, to create profound frontal impact at the street level. The 3 colours used to add luxury and aesthetic beauty and pergola was added in the design. The project was completed in three months.

Wedding Hall

Client : Mr.Arivalagan Location : Tiruvallur, Chennai

A wedding hall with grand floor space and parking feasibility and the total land area of about 4800 sq.ft. Unique and different, the elevation was designed to promise that people would think of it as a new modern wedlock building. Box type projections on the facade were used to add aesthetic beauty, to differentiate on this monolithic block and to reduce direct heat. The project is not yet started.
